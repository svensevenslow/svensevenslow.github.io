Project: Dungeon Runner

This is the first project that I have completed.
It is a platfform game created in Java and ported to Android.It has been made using the Eclipse-Ide.

Game description:

  1. The Game consists of two levels.Level 2 can be accessed only after a successful completion of Level 1.
  2. It involves guiding a sprite(character) to jump between suspended platforms and/or over obstacles to traverse their environment.
  3. The aim of the game is to collect stars in Level 1 and the golden fruit in Level 2.
  4. While attempting to collect stars and golden fruit the player also has to fight arch enemies Heliboy in Level 1 and Iron Man in Level 2.
  5. The player scores a point every time he collects a star or the golden fruit. Ideally, the game ends with successful completion of Level 2. However falling off the platform will lead to sprite death and end of the game.
  6. The weapon used by the sprite is a pistol- sword which can be used to shoot bullets
